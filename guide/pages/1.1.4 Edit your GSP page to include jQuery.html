All you have to do here is add the require to your GSP page. Anywhere will do, but in <head> makes sense, and add a bit of code:

<html>
   <head>
      <meta name="layout" content="main"/>
      <r:require modules="jquery-ui, blueprint"/>
      <r:script>
          $(function() {
              $('#form').dialog('open');
          });
      </r:script>
   </head>
   <body>
      <div id="form">
           Hello World
      </div>  
   </body>
</html>
